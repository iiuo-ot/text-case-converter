Text Case Converter

Easily change your text into uppercase, lowercase, title case, or sentence case.

Introduction

The Text Case Converter is a free online utility designed to help you change the letter case of your text easily. Whether you’re formatting titles, documents, or messages, this tool ensures your text appears exactly how you need it.

What is Text Case Converter Tool?

The Text Case Converter Tool lets you instantly switch between uppercase, lowercase, title case, and sentence case. It's especially useful for developers, writers, and students who want consistent text formatting without manual effort.

How to Use?

  • Type or paste your text in the box above.
  • Choose a case option — UPPERCASE, lowercase, Title Case, or Sentence case.
  • The converted text appears instantly in the same box.
  • Click “Copy Text” to copy the result to your clipboard.

Conclusion

The Text Case Converter simplifies your text formatting tasks. With one click, you can adjust your text’s appearance to suit professional documents, captions, or website content.
